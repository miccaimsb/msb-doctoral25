Submission Details

Complete guidelines and requirements for your doctoral consortium submission

All participants are required to submit a single PDF report using the provided LaTeX template. The report must include the following sections in order:

  • Abstract: A concise summary of your research (max 250 words).
  • PhD Research Summary: Describe your PhD project, including objectives, apporach, proposed solution, main results, etc. Maximum length: 8 pages (excluding references).
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV): Summary of your academic background, publications, and relevant experience. Maximum length: 2 pages.
  • References: Include any cited works at the end of your document.

Submission Process

  • Register: Go to the submission portal: Link coming soon
  • Receive Login Details: After registration, you will receive an email with your login credentials.
  • Upload Your Report: Log in to the portal and click on Upload Documents to submit your single PDF file.
  • Supervisor Recommendation Letter: Your supervisor will receive their own login credentials by email. They must log in to the same portal and upload a recommendation letter (PDF).

Important Notes

  • File Format: PDF only, generated using the provided LaTeX template.
  • Incomplete submissions (e.g., missing recommendation letter) will not be considered.
For any questions, please contact: miccai-student-board@csail.mit.edu
